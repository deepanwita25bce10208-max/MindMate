Statement
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Problem Statement
-----------------

Many people want to keep track of their mood and enjoy journalling about their day, but tools available online are often overly complicated, require sign-ups, or even store sensitive personal data on external servers. This project addresses the need for a simple, easy-to-run application that lets users record their emotions privately, on their own device. The aim is to make mood tracking accessible, especially for individuals who simply want to unburden their heart or keep their feelings to themselves.

--------

Scope of Project
----------------

This application mainly focuses specifically on daily mood logging. Users can write their mood and journal about their day, and have their entry automatically saved. The project is kept practical and minimalistic; focusing mainly on core Python coding and some basic GUI skills via Tkinter.

--------

Target Users
--------------
- Students who want a hassle free way to reflect on their emotional wellbeing
- Anyone who prefers an offline, no-login method for mood tracking
- Users who want full ownership of their data stored locally on their personal computer

--------

High-Level Features
-------------------
- Tkinter interface for easy navigation 
- Local JSON file used for saving and logging entries
- Timestamps for every entry
- Simple design which focuses on clarity and ease of usage
